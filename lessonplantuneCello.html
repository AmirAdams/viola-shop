Title: Quick Cello Tuning - 2 Minutes Guide Introduction: Hey cellists! Let's quickly go through how to tune your cello in just two minutes. Step 1: String Basics: Know your strings - C, G, D, A. C is low, A is high. Step 2: Get a Tuner: Grab a clip-on tuner. Attach it to your cello's scroll or bridge. Step 3: Tune the C String: Pluck C, adjust the top peg until the tuner says it's right. Step 4: Tune the G, D, A Strings: Repeat for G, D, and A. Pluck each, adjust, and hit the correct pitch. Step 5: Double-Check: Play a scale or open strings. Ensure everything sounds harmonious. Conclusion: Great job! Regular tuning keeps your cello sounding top-notch. Outro: Thanks for tuning in. Like and subscribe for more quick musical tips. Happy playing!
